Instructions

In this experiment, you will study alien words paired with unusual objects on a series of slides. Your goal is to try to remember which words refer to which objects.

After the study period, you will be asked which object goes with each word.

We are interested in how learning and memory works within healthy individuals. Please do not write anything down or use screen recordings. Just use your mind and intelligence!

During the study period, if you notice the same word-object pair repeated on consecutive trials, quickly press SPACE BAR. This is to make sure you are paying attention.
